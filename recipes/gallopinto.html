What is Gallo Pinto?

Central American essentials...

  • Rice. And. Beans.
  • Gallo Pinto is a simple dish, popular in most Central American countries, made basically with rice and beans and some greens and/or veggies of choice
  • It usually comes with one or more of these sides: eggs, suasage or other meat, sour cream, cheese, fried plantain...

Got rice and beans, what's next?

Follow these steps...

  1. Chop an onion and a bell pepper. Pick a colorful one. Fry them in some oil and add the beans, ideally with some of their own broth
  2. Cook the beans and add the rice. If you thought of adding the raw rice, just leave it here.
  3. Weirdly enough, a key ingredient is adding some Worcestershire sauce. Yeah, very central american.
  4. Mix it altogether and finally add some chopped coriander on top

Add one or two sunny side up eggs, bacon if you like, and get it on your belly!
